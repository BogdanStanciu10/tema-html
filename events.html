EVENTS

PERSONAL EVENTS

The Arcon Event Hall is the perfect setting for organizing any event in your life, whether it’s weddings, baptisms, anniversaries or any other kind of private parties.

We offer you a pleasant hall , due to its open space and upstairs floor plan. At the same time, the rectangular tables are placed so that the space is optimized to the maximum, without limiting the access of the guests. The dance floor is in the middle. The venue has a capacity of 200 seats and is ready to satisfy with its décor even the most pretentious tastes!

CAPCITIES

  • Private dining – 20
  • Restaurant seating – 120

CORPORATE EVENTS

Arcon Restaurant is located on the shores of Lake, in an exceptional natural setting.

The location, architecture and versatility of the space, along with the facilities of the event hall (video projector – upstairs, private parking, ultramodern air conditioning systems) and a team of professionals with wide experience, recommends Arcon for organizing conferences, team buildings, presentations of products or corporate Christmas parties / end of year / anniversary of the company or firm you represent.

CAPCITIES

  • Private dining – 20
  • Restaurant seating – 60
  • Conference – 18
